<TAKEDOWN>
In the year 2170, man has finally triumphed. With the combined power of the entire world, the UNITY has ended famine, disease and war.
But some resist. Hungry for power, factions have formed to throw mankind back into conflict.
Stall have declared themselves independant from the UNITY and are arming themselves with illegal equipment.
Peace is no longer an option. The rebel uprising must be taken down.
